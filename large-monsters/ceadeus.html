CEADEUS

Ceadeus is an Elder Dragon that resemble a giant whale. It has big horns that used to cause Moga earthquakes, that will grow till they overtake its eyes and make it blind. On his chest he has a luminous organs that change colors when enraged


"Legendary elder dragons known as both "shining giants of the depths" and "great sea dragons." Not confirmed to exist until recently. The Moga quakes were due to a Ceadeus butting its abnormally long horns into the earth."


Ceadeus appears in:

  • 5★ quest Save Moga Village!
  • 6★ urgent quest The Decisive Battle
