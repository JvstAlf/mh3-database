BARROTH

Barroth is a Brute Wyvern known for its particular crown structure on his head that uses do deal a lot of damage to the environment and hunters


"Barroth usually remain beneath the mud, perhaps to shield themselves from heat; they are even known to fling mud to attack. They will charge in a frenzy at anything that disturbs their bogs."


Barroth appears in:

  • 3★ quest Leading the Charge
  • 4★ quest Hunter Killer
  • 2★ Low Rank online quest Leading the Charge and Specimen Collection
  • 4★ High Rank online quests Avenging the Fallen Hunter and [Advanced] Barroth Buster
