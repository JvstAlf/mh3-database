DIABLOS

Diablos is a Flying Wyvern characterized by huge horns and a tail that end with a dangerous club. He stands on 2 legs and attacks trough terrain too.


"Wyverns feared as desert tyrants. Extremely territorial, Diablos attack invaders with blind aggression. Highly sensitive to sound, perhaps because they frequently travel beneath the sand. Known to feed on cacti."


Diablos appears in:

  • 5★ quest The Horned Dragon
  • 6★ urgent quest Four Horns
  • 3★ Low Rank online quest Into the Danger Zone
  • 3★ Low Rank online quest Wanted Alive
  • 5★ High Rank online quest Into the Danger Zone
  • 6★ High Rank online quest The Princess's Pride
